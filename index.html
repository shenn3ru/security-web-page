Stay Safe Online

Internet security is essential for protecting your personal information and devices. Always use strong passwords, update your software regularly, and avoid suspicious links.

Security is very important and should never be ignored.

Passwords like 12345 are weak. Use 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)TM or formulas like H2O.

Old advice: Use one password everywhere. New advice: Use a password manager.

As experts say: Prevention is better than cure.

Cybersecurity is not just an IT issue – it’s a life skill.

Useful Tips

1. Passwords

Use at least 12 characters, mix letters, numbers, and symbols.

2. Updates

Install patches regularly to stay protected.

3. Links

Do not click on suspicious emails or attachments.
